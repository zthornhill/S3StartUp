The Calorie Tracker Story

The Calorie Tracker Application is developed for individuals who strive to maintain healthy diets and keep a consistent watch on their daily calorie intake. As developers, we have designed a program to allow users to input each meal and track their daily calorie intake and then compare it to results found by formulas that show factors such as body mass index and metabolic rates connected to the user. This allows our users to set goals and have a reliable source to help maintain them. Premium users will have additional features to enhance their daily health such as sleep trackers and daily recipes given by local dietary professionals. Our goal at Calorie Tracker is to ensure our users have a reliable, functional platform that gives them an opportunity to live healthy lives. We understand the need for healthy diets in people of all ages, so we have designed an interphase simple enough for any type of user. We will continue to enhance the application and accompany any user wants through updates in the future.

Thank you so much for choosing Calorie Tracker for your everyday health needs. Please scroll down to meet the team behind the application foundation and development!
